    Shin Jae-ha (born April 2, 1993) is a South Korean actor.[1][2]

    Personal life[edit]

    Military service[edit]

    Shin enlisted in the mandatory military service on November 24, 2020, and he was discharged on May 23, 2022 without returning to his unit in accordance with the Korean Republican Army's policy to prevent COVID-19.[3][4]
